About Me

I'm passionate about GIS | IoT | AI, and I thrive on connecting with professionals, sharing insights, and collaborating on innovative projects. With a diverse background in automation, research and development, I bring a unique perspective to the table. I embarked on my professional journey in GIS and IT with a profound curiosity and a determination to make a meaningful impact. Over the years, I've had the privilege of navigating the dynamic landscape of technology-driven geospatial solutions. Each step of my journey has been a stepping stone toward mastering the fusion of geography and technology. These experiences have shaped me into a results-driven professional with a deep understanding of research and development (R&D) and GIS processes.

  • Name: Emile Miranda
  • Age: 28
  • Occupation: GIS Analyst and Developer at KDRM
  • Hi! I'm a geospatial data analyst and cartographer exploring geography through maps, code, and everything in between (and beyond)!
  • I am currently with KDRM
  • I live and work in beautiful Madrid, Spain

GIS, with its power to visualize and analyze spatial data, has always been at the core of my passion. My expertise in GIS spans from Esri GIS software to test autopmation. Whether it's optimizing location-based decision-making processes or developing innovative mapping solutions, I'm dedicated to harnessing GIS to drive efficiency and insight. I'm a firm believer in the transformative power of innovation. My journey has been fueled by eagerness to explore emerging trends in GIS, IoT, and AI. I'm committed to staying at the forefront of GIS and IT trends, driving change, and elevating the impact of location intelligence in diverse industries. I'm dedicated to pushing boundaries and driving positive change.

I firmly believe that meaningful connections are at the heart of professional growth. I'm open to networking opportunities, sharing insights, and collaborating on exciting projects. Whether you want to discuss the latest industry trends, explore potential partnerships, or simply connect on a professional level, please feel free to reach out.

Outside of my professional life, I'm passionate of sports like running and swimming, going around the world and exploring different places, and enjoy sacrificing my own time as a volunteer helping others. These activities not only bring balance to my life but also provide valuable perspectives that I bring to my work. I'm excited about collaboration and knowledge-sharing with the GIS and IT communities. Whether you're interested in discussing the latest GIS advancements, exploring IT-GIS integration opportunities, or simply connecting with a like-minded professional, please don't hesitate to reach out. Let's engage in meaningful discussions, explore new opportunities, and work together to make a positive impact on the world.
